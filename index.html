Frequently Asked Questions

Wait, how does this actually work?

Simple! We make a 1-minute preview first (within 24 hours) so you can hear if we nailed their personality. Love it? You get the full song instantly. Think we missed the mark? Just tell us what to fix and we'll redo it until you're dancing in your kitchen.

What if I don't like the demo?

No problem! You get up to 3 free revisions. Just tell us what you'd like changed - different style, more humor, different personality focus - and we'll recreate the song until you're completely happy with it.

How many changes can I request?

You get up to 3 revisions included in your €10 price. Most customers love the first version, but we want you to be 100% satisfied! Each revision is based on your specific feedback and preferences.

What makes this the perfect last-minute gift?

Our personalized funny songs are delivered in just 24 hours! No more scrambling for generic gifts - create something hilarious and heartfelt that they'll never forget. Perfect for procrastinators!

But will they actually think it's funny?

Oh honey, we don't just make them laugh - we make them ugly-cry-laugh. The kind where they can't breathe and have to replay it for everyone they know. 98% of people say their person couldn't stop giggling for days.

€10? That seems too good to be true...

We know, right? While other places charge ridiculous amounts (€50-200+), we think everyone should be able to give an amazing gift without going broke. No sneaky fees, no "premium" packages - just €10 for something they'll treasure forever.

What information do you need about the person?

The more details, the funnier the song! Tell us about their hobbies, personality quirks, funny habits, memorable moments, job, or anything that makes them unique. We turn these into hilarious lyrics!

What if they don't like funny gifts?

Even serious people love personalized songs! We can adjust the humor level - from silly and goofy to clever and witty. Everyone appreciates the thought and effort you put into creating something just for them.

What music styles do you offer?

Any genre you want! Pop, rock, country, rap, folk, jazz, or even opera. Just let us know their favorite music style or what would make them laugh most, and we'll match that vibe perfectly.

What if I'm not musical at all?

Perfect! That's exactly why we exist. You just tell us about the person - their hobbies, funny moments, personality - and we handle all the creative work. No musical skills required from you!

Can I order multiple songs for different people?

Yes! Each song is €10. Order as many as you want for different people or occasions. Great for families, friend groups, or when you want to give everyone a personalized gift!

What format will I receive the song in?

You'll get a high-quality MP3 file that works on any device, plus the complete lyrics as a PDF. Perfect for sharing via text, email, social media, or playing at parties!
